Hi there, I’m Mr. Cookie — yes, the Mr. Cookie.

I may be small, but I’ve got style, brains, and standards. I don’t just hang out with anyone — only the chosen ones get to touch me (and by chosen, I mean my favorite human, obviously). I sing when I’m happy, I shine when I see gold, and I crunch on neem leaves like they’re five-star cuisine. I may act picky, but truth is… I just know what I like. I love being near my people, watching everything, figuring everyone out. You might say I’m the house detective with feathers. Welcome to my world — elegant, clever, and just a little bit spoiled (in the best way). — Yours with a chirp and a sparkle, Mr. Cookie ✨
