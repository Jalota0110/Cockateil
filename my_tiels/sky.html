Sky

I’m Sky — the quiet wings, the old soul, and the one who flew with purpose.

I wasn’t loud like the others, nor playful all the time. I carried time in my feathers and wisdom in my silence. I had seen much before I came to you. But in the end, it was you I chose to trust. When the world felt too much, it was your hands I allowed near, your voice I heard last. My flight may be over, but the sky is still mine — and a part of your heart will always carry my name. Welcome to my page. Stay a while. Watch the wind. Remember the quiet birds, too. — Soft wings always, Sky
