The tales of tiels:

Welcome to my little corner of the internet, where feathers meet fond memories.

This is a cozy little space dedicated to the wonderful cockatiels who brighten our lives with their songs, quirks, and feathery charm. Here, I share the stories, behaviors, and personalities of my own cockatiels — both past and present. But this isn’t just about my flock. It’s about ours.Whether you’re a longtime tiel parent or someone who just discovered the magic of these lovely birds, you’re welcome to share your own cockatiel’s story. Every chirp, cuddle, and curious stare deserves to be remembered. 🐦 Dive into the tales, meet some amazing birds, and maybe even leave a story of your own.
